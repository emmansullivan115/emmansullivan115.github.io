Personal Growth


Harder, Better, Faster

"As college students, think of how much of your time energy and effort is dedicated to making sure your body matches the standards of our society, making sure your clothes help you fall into the social bracket that you want, making sure your hair and make up are always looking better and better. Could you possibly compare the number of hours spent in the gym to the number of hours you spent working on your true self?"
